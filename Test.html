Who Are We?

Hello! We're **Zwelakhe Mazibuko** and **Phozisa Diko**, two passionate oral hygiene students from **Wits University, Johannesburg**. We started Oral Health Heroes because we believe that good oral health education should begin early and be accessible to everyone.

Currently, our focus is entirely on **non-invasive dental screenings** for schools and other community groups. We're still building our foundation, learning, and constantly improving our service to ensure every screening is a positive and educational experience for the children.

In the future, once we've graduated and are fully established, we're excited to expand our services to include comprehensive treatments like cleanings, fluoride applications, fissure sealants, and more. For now, we're dedicated to making a difference one smile at a time through preventative care.

Our Services

Non-Invasive Dental Screenings for Schools

Our primary service focuses on providing thorough, yet gentle, dental screenings for students directly at their school premises. We identify potential oral health issues early, offer basic oral hygiene education, and provide recommendations for further care if needed.

  • Visual examination of teeth and gums.
  • Assessment for common dental issues (e.g., cavities, gingivitis).
  • Basic oral hygiene instruction tailored for children.
  • Recommendations for parents/guardians on next steps.
  • All screenings are conducted by qualified Wits University Oral Hygiene students.

We do not perform any invasive procedures during our screenings.
